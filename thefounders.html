Samer Alfayad

Full professor in Mechatronics Systems at UPSACLAY.

From 2011 to 2019, he was Associate Professor in humanoid robotics at UVSQ, holding at the same time an industrial excellence chair in Robotics. From 2010 to 2011, he was a Post-doc researcher at TUM-Germany, with a scholarship from the Alexander Von Humboldt foundation.

He received his Ph.D in robotics from UVSQ in 2009. Awarded the best Ph.D thesis in robotics by the French CNRS in 2010, and also the best Ph.D thesis in robotics for the 20th anniversary of UVSQ.

Leader of the mechatronics research group at hCode and UPSACLAY. Team leader of the Paris-Saclay team at the Mohamed Bin Zayed International Robotics Challenge (MBZIRC2017). Co-coordinator of the Institute for Control and Decision (iCode Paris-Saclay). Published 28 peer-reviewed original publications and 14 patents, obtaining €2.5M of funding over the last 5 years.

Jean-Claude RASSOU

CEO (Ingénieur EFREI, Executive MBA ESSEC).

Jean-Claude is the former CEO of Motorola France. He led an international R&D team of 600 engineers, with a budget of €160M. Today, he brings his extensive expertise in management, innovation, and business leadership to KALYSTA.
