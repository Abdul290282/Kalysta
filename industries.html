Les DeepTech

Empowering deep-tech startups.
(Disruptive or disruptive start-ups (disruptive startups or deep tech startups in English) are young companies developing techniques considered to be highly innovative.)

Viva Technology

Europe's biggest startup and tech event.
(Viva Technology, or VivaTech, is an annual event dedicated to technological innovation and start-ups created in 2016. It is held annually at the Paris Expo Porte de Versailles, in Paris, France, and is organized by the Les Échos and Publicis groups1. The first three days of VivaTech are exclusively for start-ups, investors, executives, students and academics. The event is open to the general public on the fourth day2.)
