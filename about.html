About KALYSTA

KALYSTA is a French start-up, spun off from the Paris-Saclay University Robotics Lab. KALYSTA develops high-performance, innovative robots and mechatronic systems, focusing on:

  • High power in a compact, lightweight design
  • Optimized energy consumption
  • Simplified architecture, for easy control by external AI systems

Founded in 2021 as a deep-tech startup at Paris-Saclay University, KALYSTA aims to design and manufacture cutting-edge mechatronic solutions. Our foundations include:

  • 20 years of research
  • 13 researchers specializing in various fields of robotics
  • 9 international patents
  • Approximately €0.5 M in pre-orders
  • 1 Chair in Industrial Excellence in Mechatronics Design in partnership with UEVE/UPsaclay
